To add a JAR to a project in NetBeans:

1)  Open up the "Project Properties" dialog either by clicking File->Project Properites or
    right-clicking on the project name in the "Projects" panel and selecting "Properites" at the
    bottom of the menu.

2)  Select the "Libraries" option (to the left)

3)  Make sure the "Compile" tab is selected, and click Add JAR/Folder

4)  Navigate to your JAR file and click "Open"

5)  Once the JAR appears in the "Compile-Time Libraries" list, click "Okay".
